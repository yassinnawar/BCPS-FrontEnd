About Our Service

Breast Cancer Prediction System is a helpful and useful system to people.
According to the World Health Organization, 2.3 million people were diagnosed with breast cancer in 2020 and 685,000 deaths were due to breast cancer. If detected early, “Breast Cancer is more likely to respond to treatment and can be operated on and results in a greater probability of survival and less morbidity” (WHO 2021). This project aims to improve the Breast cancer prediction in a human body based on an input of symptoms or an image CT scan/Mammogram that will be compared to a dataset collected over the years with the help of different researchers and medical professionals.
The objectives of this project are to firstly, understand the medical domain, collect different datasets of breast cancer, secondly, to preprocess the datasets collected, then after preprocessing, analytic Machine/Deep Learning techniques will be applied. Finally, the last step is to validate and evaluate the achieved results.
Our hope is to help patients predict their diagnosis early and give them a better chance for a better life.
